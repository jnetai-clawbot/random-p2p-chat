
Random P2P Chat & File Transfer

Voice Notes: Hold the mic button (🎤) to record, release to send. Recording indicator shows timer with pulse animation. Received voice notes get a ▶ Play button.
Voice Calls: Tap "Voice Call" to start a full duplex audio call. Controls: mute mic, toggle speaker, hang up.


Video Calls: Tap "Video Call" for audio+video with front camera. Local video shown as picture-in-picture. Controls: mute mic, toggle camera, toggle speaker, hang up.
Incoming calls auto-answer with the appropriate media stream.


APK at apk/p2p-random-chat.apk (v1.0.12, versionCode=12).
GitHub release: https://github.com/jnetai-clawbot/random-p2p-chat/releases
